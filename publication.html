    • Books / Theses

    Praveen Babu Choppala is an author or co-author of 6 books. His publications include "Recent Developments in Reducing Random Valued Impulse Noise in Color Images - The need for speed," which delves into image processing, and "Seven Innovative Technologies Towards Human Advancement and Smart Living," exploring technology's impact on society. Other works include "Filtering methods to remove noise in digital colour images," focusing on image clarity, "Probability Theory and Random Processes," a theoretical exploration, "Fundamentals of Image Processing," another contribution to the field of image analysis, and "Bayesian multiple target tracking," which covers advanced tracking methodologies.
