    Co-Curricular Activities

    Praveen Babu Choppala has demonstrated a strong commitment to co-curricular engagement throughout his career. Notably, he took a pioneering initiative in organizing online project vivas for outgoing B.Tech. students in Andhra Pradesh during the challenging times of the April 2020 COVID-19 pandemic. This effort ensured the timely course completion for students despite the lockdown. He also served as the President of the ECE Department Day at Wellfare Engineering College in November 2022, where he ideated, organized, and chaired the event, which saw a large turnout of students and parents. Furthermore, Choppala coordinated the Vijay Diwas Program at Welfare Engineering College in December 2021, commemorating India’s victory over Pakistan in 1971. He also organized a Paper Bag Day at Wellfare Engineering College to raise awareness about the harmful impact of plastic bags, which included an innovative auction sale of paper bags made by students. Additionally, he coordinated experiential learning activities for the Signals and Systems course at ANITS, facilitating students in developing real online signal processing projects.
