    • Introduction


    Hello, I am Praveen Babu Choppala, a dedicated and accomplished professional with a strong background in engineering and computer science. I hold a PhD in Engineering and Computer Science, along with a Master's in VLSI Design and a Bachelor's in Electronics & Communication Engineering. Throughout my career, I have focused on scientific thought and multidisciplinary research, demonstrating my ability to unravel complex challenges. My academic summary includes significant achievements such as authoring 6 books, securing 6 patents, and contributing to numerous peer-reviewed publications. I also have 88 Google Scholar citations and an h-index of 6. Professionally, I have held various positions, including Professor at Wellfare Institute of Science Technology and Management, Associate Professor at Anil Neerukonda Institute of Technology and Science, and Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of New South Wales, Sydney. I am passionate about applying my expertise to create innovative solutions and have been involved in various research consultancy projects, including the development of smart systems and automation technologies. I am a Senior Member of the IEEE and have been actively involved in professional activities, including serving as Chairman of Board of Studies and Head of the Department. My goal is to continue making meaningful contributions to my field through research, teaching, and innovation.
